# Retail Customer Segmentation & Purchase Behaviour Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyses retail transaction and customer behaviour data to identify high-value customer segments, product preferences, and sales opportunities. The goal is to support data-driven retail strategy by understanding which customer groups contribute most to sales, what products they prefer, and how purchasing behaviour differs across customer segments.

## Business Problem

Retail businesses need to understand customer purchasing behaviour to improve marketing, product placement, and promotional strategies. This project focuses on identifying the most valuable customer segments and the products that drive sales performance.

## Key Business Questions

- Which customer segments generate the highest total sales?
- Which lifestage groups contribute most to purchasing activity?
- Do budget, mainstream, or premium customers spend more?
- Which product brands and pack sizes perform best?
- Which customer groups should be targeted for future promotions?
- What purchasing patterns can support better retail strategy?

## Dataset Description

The project uses two retail datasets:

- Transaction data: includes sales transactions, product details, quantities, and total sales.
- Customer behaviour data: includes customer lifestage and premium customer category.

The datasets were cleaned, prepared, and merged using Python/Pandas before analysis.

## Project Workflow

1. Loaded transaction and customer behaviour datasets.
2. Performed data cleaning using Python/Pandas.
3. Checked missing values, duplicates, and data types.
4. Extracted product brand names and pack sizes from product names.
5. Merged transaction data with customer behaviour data.
6. Exported cleaned datasets for SQL and Power BI analysis.
7. Used SQL for validation and business analysis queries.
8. Built a Power BI dashboard to present customer and product insights.
9. Created business recommendations based on analysis findings.

## Key Analysis Areas

### Customer Segmentation Analysis
Analysed customer behaviour across lifestage and premium customer categories to identify the most valuable customer groups.

### Product Performance Analysis
Examined top-selling products, brands, and pack sizes based on total sales and quantity sold.

### Sales Behaviour Analysis
Reviewed sales contribution, purchase frequency, and average spend across customer segments.

### Business Recommendations
Provided recommendations for targeted promotions, product strategy, and customer segment prioritisation.
